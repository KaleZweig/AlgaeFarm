Open Algae Farm

The Modular Algae Farming model was developed using a number of different materials which are both efficient and could be transferred to a rooftop scale. For the design we created a network of vinyl tubing which would circulate, harvest and drain our chlorella algae. The model pursues this windy appearance as we wanted to maximize the surface area thus allowing for the algae to gain ample exposure to sunlight while utilizing more efficient quantities of roofing. In addition to the vinyl tubing we have created a series of custom designed and printed tubing joints/connectors followed by a pump for circulation and a solenoid which allows us to drain the harvested algae. To conclude the description of the design, we have used a 2’ by 3’ section of plywood that resembles a section of everyday rooftop in which our design would be placed.
